About Us

Born in Pavia, Italy, from the 40-year-long tradition of the Biomedical Informatics Lab "Mario Stefanelli", Bilobe is the melting pot of cutting-edge academic research and real-world implementation in healthcare. Our journey began with a vision: to harness the transformative potential of artificial intelligence to tackle the complex challenges of human health and healthcare delivery.

Our mission is to support healthcare practitioners, enhance patient outcomes, and improve healthcare organizations with innovative technology. We excel in harnessing unstructured data to turn complex information into actionable insights through transparent and trustworthy solutions.

By combining advanced algorithms with our medical domain know-how, we aim to make healthcare more efficient, accessible, and personalized. Join us on our journey to redefine healthcare turning data into information and actionable insights.
