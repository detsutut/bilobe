About Us

Born from 40 years of biomedical informatics research.

location_on Pavia, Italy

Born in Pavia, Italy from the 40-year tradition of the Biomedical Informatics Lab "Mario Stefanelli", Bilobe is the melting pot of cutting-edge academic research and real-world implementation in healthcare.

Our mission is to support healthcare practitioners, enhance patient outcomes, and improve healthcare organizations with innovative technology — turning complex, unstructured data into actionable insights through transparent and trustworthy solutions.

By combining advanced algorithms with deep medical domain expertise, we make healthcare more efficient, accessible, and personalized.
